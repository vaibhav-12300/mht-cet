📋 General Instructions

  1. This examination consists of 100 questions carrying 1 mark each.
  2. Total time allotted: 120 Minutes (2 Hours).
  3. Each correct answer awards +1 mark. There is NO Negative Marking.
  4. The exam is divided into 3 sections: Mathematics (50Q), Computer Science & IT (30Q), and Logical Reasoning (20Q).
  5. You can navigate to any question at any time during the exam.
  6. Do NOT refresh the page during the examination.
  7. The exam will auto-submit when the timer reaches 00:00:00.

🖱️ How to Attempt a Question

  1. Click on the option (A/B/C/D) to select your answer.
  2. Click "Save & Next" to save the answer and move to the next question.
  3. Click "Mark for Review & Next" to flag the question and move forward.
  4. Click "Clear Response" to deselect your answer for that question.
  5. Use the Question Palette on the right to jump directly to any question.
